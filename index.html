Welcome To Royal Sweetness


We offer a wide variety of cakes and cupcakes, cookies, bars, pies, pastries, tarts and other groovy creations. In addition to our regular menu, we also offer many of our baked goods in half size, quarter size and miniature to accomodate for larger crowds. Come to the Groovy Duck and sit down on some of our groovy repurposed furniture while you enjoy a latte and a muffin. Bring the family, we don't mind! We have a small area where small children can sit at their own little table and play games and have fun. No time to relax? Not to worry! We have easy access and convenient parking. We can get you in and out in a flash....even faster when you pre-order large orders!
